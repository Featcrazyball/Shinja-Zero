About

She was the chief of archangels back in the heavens, and served Freya Olympus, the Goddess of Wood. She used to be in charge of training of apprentice goddesses, including Ira Olympus. Apparently, after some quarrel with the goddess, fell from the heavens.
Thousand Years Ago (New timeline)
Eriynes gathered with other Demon Kings on the North Continent, intending to intercept and defeat the Hero of Light (Abel). The fight was interrupted by Nevia Laphroaig after Takatsuki Makoto unleashed the God-Rank Water Magic, Cocytus. To save the rest of the content's population from being wiped out by Cocytus, and as a gesture of good will, Nevia placed a curse of death on the entire North Continent, forbidding anyone from harming the Hero of Light's party. This curse even affected Eriynes, a Demon King. Eriynes was angry that she came all the way from the South Continent to kill the Hero but now got cursed to not harm the Hero's party, and returned home. During the three years after Great Demon King, Iblis's defeat, Eriynes was presumably beaten by the Hero's party and sealed with power borrowed from the Tower of Babel.
