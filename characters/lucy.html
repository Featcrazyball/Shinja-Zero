About

Lucy is first seen by Makoto Takatsuki when she and her party were going to Ogre subjugation quest and aiming for Iron Rank. During their subjugation quest they ran into trouble by a stray Ogre which was more powerful than they expected. As they were escaping, Lucy tripped and fell down and couldn't escape because Ogre was already near her. Just as she was in despair of being crushed by the ogre, Makoto Takatsuki appeared and distracted the ogre, buying her precious time to escape. This ordeal caused her to feel grateful towards Makoto and she started to grow interested in him. Lucy also then became extremely good friends with Sasaki Aya.
