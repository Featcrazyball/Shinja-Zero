About

Makoto was born in Tokyo. His parents would always come back on the last train (sometimes not even that) and would always leave before he woke up. Hence, there is little to no interaction between Makoto and his parents.
Makoto is an RPG gamer freak. He likes RPG games a lot and it is implied that he only has single-player games. He is the type of player who would always go fully prepared to fight the last boss of any game.
When transported to the other world, he was looked down on by his classmates because of his weak status. Because of this, Makoto decides to adventure solo. Also, the water oracle, Sofia Eir Roses deems him not to be of any use to the country, which made him hate her and the Water Goddess, Eir.
