About

The result of the Divine Realm War was that the Holy Gods won, the Titan Gods lost, and had to be imprisoned and sealed in special places only gods could go. As for Noah, she was only imprisoned in the Deep Sea Temple, a place where mortals could go. The seal of her power would be lifted on the condition that her followers go to the Deep Sea Temple themselves and defeat the leviathan guarding that place, which is impossible for mortals. This is also a way to make her fall into despair and accept to marry Jupiter.
Noah is the youngest child of the Old Gods and proclaimed by many as the most beautiful goddess in the Divine Realm. She is one of the main heroines and one the most mysterious character as she seems to know more than what she lets on. After Takatsuki Makoto agreed to go to the past to save Hero Abel, Althena Olympus rewarded Makoto by allowing Noah to worship by the mortals of the world as one of the Eight Great Goddesses - Goddess of Beauty Noah.
At the end of the novel, Noah fell in love with Takatsuki Makoto and made him her familiar.
